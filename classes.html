    • Description: Into to Computer Programming. Basically just learning Python

    • Description: Preview of Computer Science
    • Teacher: Zoran Durich

    • Description: Calculus I
    • Teacher: Kumnit Nong.
    • Easiness (1 easy - 10 hard): 8. To be fair, I got a 100% on the first exam. However, the class average right now is like 60%, so needless to say, the class is hard.

    • Description: Reading the Arts with a focus in historical photographic manipulation.
    • Teacher: Heather Anderson (Awesome)
    • Easiness (1 easy - 10 hard): 8. The class itself is just a lecure. We have lots of readings, that tend to be very long and hard to comprehend. The real reason for the 8 is all the papers we have to write. The research paper due at the end of the year is going to be a pain
    • Interest: Kind of. The teacher and the stuff we are learning in class is cool. But I would not have taken this class unless I was forced to.
